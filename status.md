#### What have you done for your project so far?

For the most part, I believe I could be finished. I have implemented an interactable board, letters that can be selected by typing them (only if they are in the current player's letter list and still remain in the letterQuantities dictionary), appropriate scoring for whichever letter is placed, and many of the original rules of Scrabble. 

#### What have you not done for your project yet?

I am not sure yet what would be added, but I would like to think of another smaller feature or two to add.

#### What problems, if any, have you encountered?

I have really only had trouble starting the project as I did not know at first how to make the board or display text and other things. I was able to find tutorials to help me with this though so it was not a big problem. There is also a small issue I have encountered where random letters cannot be placed even when they still have at least 1 remaining in the letterQuantities dictionary, but this should also be a simple fix.
